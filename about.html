Textize An online transcript writing application
Textize was built by a team of students for the Structured Systems Analysis and Design course of International Institute of Information Technology, Hyderabad.
The team consisted of four members: Sanchit Gangwar, Abhinav Mittal, Radha Manisha, and A Rishika Reddy.
The project was to build a journal which could handle audio/video files efficiently, displaying synchronised transcripts with the audio/video. Textize was developed to facilitate the process of writing transcripts.
